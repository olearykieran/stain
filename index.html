Playwright & MELODY

Negin Poure is an Iranian-born actress, writer, and model based in New York City, making her debut as the playwright of Stain. A graduate of the American Academy of Dramatic Arts and a member of the Actors Society. Stain marks not only her first play, but the first time she steps onto the stage with her own words.

Most recent credits include: Flighty-short (Sasha), Status Quo (Isabel, Marrie), Midsummer's night dream (Helena), The Studio Ghost-short (Ghost).

www.neginpoure.com

Playwright & DYLON

Hera is a Turkish actor, artist, activist born & raised in İstanbul, now based in New York City. From a young age, Hera has always felt a strong intuition to advocate against injustices affecting people, animals, and the environment, so this project specifically means a lot to her, especially at times like these, where women's rights are under attack. She is a graduate of the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in Manhattan. In her free time, she loves to travel and paint.

www.herabarlowe.com

MR. PICKLETON

Aaron Going is an actor who was born and buttered in Billings, MT. He recently graduated as a member of the American Academy of Dramatic Arts 2024-2025 Company program and is a proud member of the Actors Society. Aaron could not be more pleased to join this team of creative juggernauts as he makes his Off-Off-Broadway debut. He hopes to bring a little more laughter and light to the world through this work. When not on set or on stage, Aaron enjoys making soup, painting, and serenading the pigeons outside his window.

www.aarongoing.com

TYLER BROOKS

ALEX VOSS

Megan Osyen is an English/Welsh actress based in New York City. Originally from London, she spent part of her childhood in Moscow and is a fluent Russian speaker. Since graduating from The American Academy of Dramatic Arts, she has appeared in a music video and five short films set to premiere at festivals in the coming year. Megan is currently in pre-production for a feature film slated for 2026, and is really looking forward to making her Off-Off-Broadway debut in "Stain", followed by a play set for December 2025.

PRODUCTION DIRECTOR

Daniely Martins is an actress, writer and director, born and raised in Brazil and now based in New York City. She has always had the passion for moving people through words and a need for physical expression. Her pull towards discoveries brought her to live in Portugal, Germany and France, where she collected a great deal of experiences in human connection and theater studies. Daniely's most recent credits include the direction of Someone ExtraordinariX at The Players Theater and her performance in the devised project titled Night Witches, by the PickPocket Players. Her training consists in the two year conservatory program at The American Academy of Dramatic Arts, in New York, and the three year extensive Acting in English program at Cours Florent, in Paris, France.
